Winter

It was a very cold winter day when I went to the Met and wanted to draw something out of real life. A bronze statue caught my eyes right away -- it was a girl wrapping herself in a thick scarf. Her eyes closed, her back slight bent, hugged tightly to keep her body warm.

Grandma I

I once watched an old Chinese movie called “Postmen in the Mountains” (那山，那人，那狗). There was a scene, in which, this old lady, blind, was waiting for her grandson's mail everyday. The postman delievered the letter, and she asked if he could read it for her. She sit there quietly; the light came through the window and brightened her face. No word was spoken, but a thousand thoughts ran through my mind.
