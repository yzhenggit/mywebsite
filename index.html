Miller Postdoctoral Fellow
Miller Institute For Basic Research In Science, UC Berkeley, CA

Hello! I'm currently a Miller Fellow in Department of Astronomy at UC Berkeley. I'm interested in galaxy formation and evolution, baryon cycles between galaxies and their circum-galactic medium (CGM), including inflows, outflows, and gas recycling. My research focuses on constructing a coherent picture of how baryons cycle and recycle between galaxies and their CGM using multi-wavelength spectroscopy (UV Spectroscopy, HI 21cm, IFU, etc.) and cosmological simulations (Enzo & FOGGIE).

After work, I enjoy climbing, cycling, and yoga. I also like to record things in drawing beside telescope CCDs. Please check out MY ARTWORK page for some fun sketches!
